About Chain Naturopathy

Established with the aim of realizing the great goal of Medicine Free India, "Chain Naturopathy" is working in the field of health services in Hisar, Haryana, it is different from other institutions. Often these institutions take recourse to the medicines of a particular medical system like Ayurvedic, Allopathy or Homeopathy. Some organizations are also working in the field of healthy living without medicine through different means like yoga, naturopathy, magnet therapy, acupressure etc.

The aim of "Chain Naturopathy" is to integrate all the methods of healthy living without giving medicine. The aim of the organization is to do new research in these areas and to make them accessible to the masses. Right now this organization is working only in the field of Naturopathy, Yoga and Acupressure. In future, other methods of treatment without giving medicine will also be brought under its scope.

The people associated with the organization may not get complete freedom from the medicine yet, but their dependence on the medicine will definitely reduce.
